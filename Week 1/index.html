Introduction

Hi! I am Aditya Harkare, a 2028 batch Civil Engineering Student at IIT Bombay. It's been a year since I joined the 'insti' , had a lot of new experiences, learning new things, met a lot of people and made great friends. I'm going to share my experiences and glimpses of my first year at the "Insti".

The first day at Insti, the rain, the greenery along with the excitement and enthuciasm in the convocation hall.

Memories of celebrating festivals together. Playing games and studying all night.

Makerspace Project

The feeling of accomplishment of making something on your own. The teamwork and satisfaction after seeing the drone working

Getting mesmerised by Robowars,competitions,exhibitions and the drone show.
